Effective Date: July 14, 2026


Thank you for using Eight Puzzle. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled while using the application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Collect

Eight Puzzle does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Location Data
  • Contacts
  • Camera Data
  • Microphone Data
  • Payment Information
  • Photos
  • Messages
  • Device Identifiers

2. Permissions

Eight Puzzle does not require access to sensitive device permissions such as camera, contacts, microphone, phone, SMS, or location.

3. Offline Functionality

Eight Puzzle is a completely offline puzzle game. No gameplay information is transmitted to any server.

4. Advertisements

Eight Puzzle contains no advertisements and does not integrate any advertising SDKs.

5. Analytics

The application does not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or any third-party tracking services.

6. Data Sharing

Because no personal information is collected, no user information is shared, sold, rented, or transferred to third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Eight Puzzle is suitable for users of all ages. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

8. Local Data Storage

Game statistics such as Best Time, Best Moves, Games Played, Games Won, Average Time, Average Moves, and Win Rate are stored only on your device to improve gameplay. No information is uploaded to external servers.

9. Data Security

Since no personal information is collected or stored online, your privacy remains protected while using the application.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the application. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
